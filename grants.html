Grants

R & D Project, Ministry of Science & Technology, 2025

Proposed: "A Digital Waste Tracking System" (Under review)

Proposed and designed a national-level research initiative to develop:

  • A mobile app for real-time waste collection tracking.
  • A web dashboard for municipal authorities to monitor waste management efficiency.
  • Citizen reporting tools to flag illegal dumping sites via geotagged images.

Objective: Reduce urban waste backlog in Dhaka by 30% through data-driven route optimization.

Innovation Fund, ICT Innovation Fund, 2025

Proposed: "Smart Urban Agriculture with IoT and AI" (Under review)

Led proposal development for an IoT- and AI-driven farming system in Narayanganj District, targeting:

  • IoT sensors for soil moisture, nutrient, and microclimate monitoring.
  • AI-based predictive models to optimize irrigation, fertilization, and pest control.
  • Mobile alerts for farmers to mitigate crop risks (e.g., drought, disease outbreaks).

Goal: Increase smallholder farmer income by 20% while reducing water/fertilizer waste.
