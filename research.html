Research

  • M.S.c Thesis: Enhanced Detection of GAN‑Generated Image Forgeries Using Error Level Analysis and Deep Learning Models

    Jahangirnagar University, Fall 2024

    Advisor: Dr. M. Mesbahuddin Sarker, Professor [Google Scholar]

    Department of Information Technology, Jahangirnagar University, Savar, Dhaka

    Developed a model combining Error Level Analysis(ELA) with deep learning to detect GAN‑generated image forgeries. Utilized a dataset of 8,020 images, achieving superior accuracy with Xception, which outperformed other models in forgery detection.

  • B.S.c Thesis: BonoNet: A Deep Convolutional Neural Network for Recognizing Bangla Compound Characters

    Daffodil International University, Fall 2022

    Advisor: Nusrat Jahan, Assistant Professor & Head [Google Scholar]

    Department of Information Technology & management, Daffodil International University, Savar, Dhaka

    Developed BonoNet, a deep CNN model for recognizing Bangla compound characters, utilizing the AIBangla dataset with 171 classes. Achieved the highest accuracy, outperforming existing models and enhancing Bangla OCR systems. This advancement supports the automation and analysis of compoundBangla characters.
